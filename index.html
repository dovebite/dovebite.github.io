WELCOME TO MY NEST

You have wandered into Dove's little corner of the world wide web! I wanted to build this website as a hub for all the things that have to do with me as well as a collection of little things I see and like and want to share with the world. We exist in cyberspace just as much as we do in the 'real world'. This is my way of carving out a little existence for myself.

I want to say I build this website as a way to consolidate my online presence a bit, but honestly, this still only consodilates a small part of it. I wanted to build this website as an exercise of HTML and CSS as well as an exercise is attemtpting to consolidate the many facets of who I am as a person and how I exist in cyberspace.
